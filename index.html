About ISEC

ISEC is a student-led initiative dedicated to creating meaningful opportunities for young minds across India. Through debates, Model United Nations conferences, leadership summits, public speaking workshops and collaborative learning experiences, ISEC empowers students to discover their voice, strengthen their abilities and prepare for the future with confidence.
